The Bellevue Table Tennis Club (BTTC) is a non-profit organization that provides table tennis equipment such as tables, nets, and balls to local players with a small amount of charge. The goal of the club is to create a friendly environment for table tennis players of all levels and ages. We also hope to share our love of the sport with members of the local community.
